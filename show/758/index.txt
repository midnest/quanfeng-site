</p>
<p>

Oil bearing: the AC fan with oil bearing mainly relies on the mutual friction between stator and coil, and the oil in the bearing is gradually reduced with the use of fan, non-stop operation and time consumption, resulting in the wind speed becoming slower and slower when using the fan.
</p>

</p>
